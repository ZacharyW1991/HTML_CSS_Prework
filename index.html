In search of an aesthetic

Here's the question: do we define our aesthetic? Or it defined by our experiences? For some it is one or the other, some it is both, yet many have none at all.

So what?

Nobody truly has a coherent aesthetic if you look closely enough. One can enjoy a variety of interests from old theater to military hardware, literature to videogames, Baroque paintings to Beat Poetry.

Away from the nihilistic

Nonetheless, by no means should aesthetics be neglected. Beauty may be in the eye of the beholder, but it also inspires one to be the best one can be. Apathy is death.

Yep, that's me. You're probably wondering how I got here. So am I.
